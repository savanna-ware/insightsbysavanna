Hello! I'm Savanna! I'm a business analyst at Société Générale and soon-to-be graduate in Data Science and Business Intelligence. I grew up in the US and have spent the last several years living and working in France, an experience that has shaped how I think about communication, culture, and complexity.

My background began in psychology research, where I learned to observe carefully, question assumptions, and turn behavioral data into structured insight. That foundation travels with me into every business problem I work on today.

I love using data to extract the story beneath the surface whether that's mapping a process that isn't working or understanding why people behave the way they do.

I'm looking for my next opportunity in data/business analysis where I can keep learning and make the invisible visible through data.
